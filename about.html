Who We Are

A team of passionate engineers and conservationists dedicated to protecting wildlife

The Future of Wildlife Monitoring

ConservaCam combines motion-sensing hardware and AI-driven software to capture, analyze, and transmit biodiversity data. Our system identifies individual animals, tracks behaviors, and provides actionable insights to support conservation efforts.

Our Approach

We leverage cutting-edge technology to provide conservancies with the tools they need for effective wildlife monitoring and protection. Our solution is built on years of research and field testing.

Our Impact

Successfully deployed in pilot projects, including Nyeri County, with measurable results. Our technology is trusted by conservationists and researchers for delivering accurate and impactful data.

Our Purpose and Aspirations

Our Mission

To empower conservancies with advanced tools for monitoring, understanding, and protecting biodiversity.

Our Vision

A world where technology harmonizes with nature to preserve ecosystems for future generations.
